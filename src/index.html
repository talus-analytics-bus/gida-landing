The GEORGETOWN INFECTIOUS DISEASE ATLAS is an integrated research platform providing quick access to a comprehensive set of global health security visualization dashboards, decision support tools, and data libraries developed by the Center for Global Health Science and Security at Georgetown University. See below for a description of each tool, links to the sites, and access to a quick download of the data from each.

WEBSITES AND TOOLS
GLOBAL HEALTH SECURITY TRACKING
GHSAURL.COM
img here

The Global Health Security Tracking site is an integrated mapping and data visualization platform showing the flow of funding for global health security from funder to recipient globally. Funds are categorized by their intended impact on improving health security, including funds targeted for specific outbreak response efforts.

Katz, R., E. Graeden, J. Kerr, S. Eaneff (2019). Tracking the flow of funds in global health security. EcoHealth. 1-8.
